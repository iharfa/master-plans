Framework ↔ Master Plans

Are the plans pulling in the same direction?

Every Strategic Policy Direction in the Urban Development Framework should be carried by an action in a master plan — with no two plans quietly duplicating or contradicting each other. This workbench checks that, and names the document to change when they drift.

Coverage by pillar

Share of each pillar's objectives that at least one plan contributes a delivering action toward.

Read this as a structural / textual alignment aid, not an engineering or legal review. The curated Gaps, Overlaps and By-Document tabs are the substantive findings; the keyword engine on Upload & Versions is a transparent change-tracker for revisions.

Tools

Upload revised plans & track versions

Drop in updated .md plans. The engine re-checks each gap, diffs against the previous run, and version-tracks the result — all in your browser.

How to read the engine. It scans each plan for the distinctive signal phrases of the 8 tracked gaps. A match means the gap topic now appears in the text — a prompt to confirm it's a real delivering action, not just narrative. The authoritative, action-level judgement stays the curated figures on the other tabs; the engine shows what changed between revisions.
